About Us

Our Story in the Treetops

Founded in 2024, The Canopy Lounge is a cedar-plank treehouse cafe wrapped around a living oak, 40 feet above the Western Ghats. We built every beam by hand — no nails through the bark — so the tree keeps growing while you dine.

🌲 Environment

Floor-to-ceiling windows, forest mist, and birdsong at sunrise. A calm escape from the city.

☕ Quality

Single-origin coffee, farm-fresh ingredients, and recipes inspired by Indian and global flavours.

🍽️ Services

Dine-in, deck seating, private events, takeaway, and table-side service from our canopy waiter.

✨ Specialties

Filter kaapi, masala chai, wood-fired wraps, and our famous Sunset Deck Platter for two.
